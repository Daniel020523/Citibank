FAQ
How do I reset my PIN?
Go to Settings → Security → Change PIN. You will need your current PIN or an OTP sent to your registered number.
How long do transfers take?
Citibank-to-Citibank transfers are instant. Other bank transfers via NIP are completed within 30 minutes.
What are the transfer limits?
Daily transfer limit is ₦5,000,000 for verified accounts. Contact support to request a higher limit.
How does saved card payment work?
When you add a card, Citibank tokenizes it via Paystack and stores a secure authorization code. Future payments use this code — no OTP or PIN required.
